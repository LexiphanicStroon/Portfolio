My Background

I worked for seven years in the construction industry, travelling often and learning a lot.
It wasn't what I wanted to do forever, and Computer Science was my strongest subject in high school,

However, I learned a lot from the trades. How important strong communication is, performing well under stress,
working long hours, and the list goes on. My experience is invaluable, and I don't regret it in the slightest.
I took a huge leap when I decided to change careers, and enrolled in Web Development with Robertson College.

I studied hard, and graduated with honours with no prior experience with higher learning.
I think the most important thing a student of development can learn, is HOW to learn.

Above all, being able to solve problems, and adapt to new environments are the most important lessons I've learned.
